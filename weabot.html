Weabot

About

It is an environmental puzzle game titled "Weabot." The main character is a small flying drone with the ability to take control of other robots encountered along its path, using them to overcome environmental puzzles that block its way.

Project Info

Team Size: 14

Time Frame: 6 Months

Engine: Unreal Engine 5
